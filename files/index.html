About us

AngelPM was founded by investors frustrated with the difficulties of keeping track of startup investments and portfolio companies.

Paper and pdf deal documents are unwieldy, making it hard to keep track of terms like discounts and caps, maturity dates and ownership percentages. Additionally, many startups are bad at keeping investors informed about progress.

AngelPM solves both problems by presenting key deal information as well as aggregating company updates.
